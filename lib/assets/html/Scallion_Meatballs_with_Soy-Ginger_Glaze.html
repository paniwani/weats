Scallion Meatballs with Soy-Ginger Glaze

From smittenkitchen.com

Photo © Deb Perelman

  • 1/2 cup mirin (sweet rice wine), or 1/2 cup sake with 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup peeled, chopped ginger
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
